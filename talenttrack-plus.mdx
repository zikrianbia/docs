## What is TalentTrack+?

### Attract. Develop. Retain.

TalentTrack+ is an integrated workforce platform designed to help organisations build long-term workforce resilience through skills-based talent management. It supports the full talent lifecycle by enabling companies to:

* **Attract** the right people based on skills
* **Develop** employees through targeted upskilling
* **Retain** talent by supporting meaningful career growth

## Part of Singapore’s Career Health Initiative

TalentTrack+ is part of Singapore’s national **Career Health** initiative, championed by **SkillsFuture Singapore (SSG)**.

The initiative was introduced to ensure that enterprises and workers remain competitive in a rapidly changing economy shaped by technological disruption and evolving job demands. TalentTrack and TalentTrack+ were launched to equip organisations with structured, data-driven tools to strengthen workforce readiness.

## Designed for SMEs and Growing Enterprises

Small and medium-sized enterprises are the backbone of Singapore’s economy. However, they often face challenges in:

* Attracting skilled talent
* Retaining high-performing employees
* Planning structured career development
* Identifying emerging skill needs

TalentTrack+ levels the playing field by providing SMEs with capabilities traditionally available only to larger corporations. It enables organisations to proactively manage talent using skills data and workforce insights.

## Powered by JobTech

TalentTrack+ is powered by **JobTech**, the official technology partner appointed by SkillsFuture Singapore and Talented, JobTech's flagship product for developing and managing talent.

The platform combines:
* A large-scale labour market data engine
* Employer and employee tools for skills profiling and workforce planning
* Industry and sector-level dashboards
* Skills-based matching and recommendations

The platform is integrated with **Singpass** and the **Career & Skills Passport**, enabling secure retrieval of certified skills data. This ensures high levels of data accuracy and trust.

## Why TalentTrack+ Matters

In a world where roles evolve rapidly and new skills emerge constantly, organisations need visibility into:

1. What skills their workforce currently has
2. Where critical skill gaps exist
3. Which roles are evolving
4. What training investments are required

TalentTrack+ provides structured, actionable insights that help organisations make informed decisions across attraction, development, and retention.
