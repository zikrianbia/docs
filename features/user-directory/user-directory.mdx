The User Directory is a central directory listing all employees and their skill profiles. Use it to discover experts, view colleague profiles, and find the right people for projects or collaboration.

## Browsing the directory

When you open the User Directory, you will see a list of all employees in the organisation.

[Upload Image 1 here]

Each entry displays:

* **Name** and email address
* **Workday ID** — The employee's unique identifier
* **Current role** — Their job title within the organisation

---

## Employee skill profile

Click on any employee to view their detailed skill profile.

[Upload Image 2 here]

The profile includes:

* **Skills list** — All skills in the employee's profile
* **Proficiency levels** — Each skill is displayed with a proficiency rating ranging from **Introductory** to **Expert**
* **Manager's Rating** — The manager's assessment alongside the self-assessed rating

---

## Search and filter

Use the search bar and filters to find specific employees or expertise.

[Upload Image 3 here]

* **Search** by employee name, job title, or skill keyword
* **Filter** by department, location, or other criteria
* Results update dynamically as you refine your search

<Tip>
  Use the User Directory to find subject matter experts when you need guidance on a specific topic, or to discover potential mentors for your development plan.
</Tip>
