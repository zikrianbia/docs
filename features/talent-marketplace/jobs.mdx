The Jobs module within the Talent Marketplace helps you discover internal job openings tailored to your skills and career aspirations. Browse recommended roles, apply directly, or refer opportunities to colleagues.

## Explore recommendations

When you open the Jobs module, you will see job recommendations based on your current skills and career aspirations.

[Upload Image 1 here]

Recommendations are organised into two categories:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Based on your current skills" icon="bullseye">
    Roles that match the skills already in your profile. These are positions where you have a strong alignment today.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Based on your aspired roles" icon="rocket">
    Roles that align with the career aspirations you selected during onboarding or updated in your profile.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

Each job card displays:

* **Job title** and department
* **Match score** indicator (e.g. Strong)
* **Location** and vacancy count
* **Bookmark icon** to save for later

<Tip>
  If you want to see different recommendations, click **Explore more roles** to update your career aspirations and get personalised suggestions.
</Tip>

## Browse all jobs

Switch to the **Browse All Jobs** tab to view the full catalogue of available positions across the organisation.

[Upload Image 2 here]

* **Search** by job title, business unit, or keyword
* **Filter** by Business Unit, Department, Location, and Rank
* **Sort** by creation date (newest to oldest, or oldest to newest)
* Navigate through results using **pagination** controls

---

## Job details

Click on any job card to view the full job details page.

[Upload Image 3 here]

The detail page includes:

* **Job title and match score** — See how well your skills align with the role
* **In-demand skills** — Skills required for the role, shown as skill pills
* **Skills you already have** (✓) and **Skills you want to develop** (★) — A clear breakdown comparing your profile to the job requirements
* **Job description** — Full role overview, responsibilities, and acceptance criteria
* **Notes before applying** — Additional information from the hiring manager

### Quick actions

From the job detail page, you can:

* **Share** the job URL with others
* **Add to bookmark** to save it for later
* **Apply / Refer a friend** to submit your application or share the role
