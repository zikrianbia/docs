The Jobs module within the Talent Marketplace helps you discover internal and external job openings tailored to your skills and career aspirations. Browse recommended roles, apply directly, or refer opportunities to colleagues.

## Explore recommendations

When you open the Jobs module, you will see job recommendations based on your current skills and career aspirations.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-41.png)
</Frame>

Recommendations are organised into two categories:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="bullseye" title="Based on your current skills">
    Roles that match the skills already in your profile. These are positions where you have a strong alignment today.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="rocket" title="Based on your aspired roles">
    Roles that align with the career aspirations you selected during onboarding or updated in your profile.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Job details

Click on any job card to view the full job details page.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-44.png)
</Frame>

### Quick actions

From the job detail page, you can:

- **Share** the job URL with others
- **Add to bookmark** to save it for later
- **Apply / Refer a friend** to submit your application or share the role

---

## Share a job post

You can share a job opportunity with colleagues in two ways:

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-45.png)
</Frame>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Refer by name">
    Select a friend's name from the dropdown to refer the job directly to them.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Copy link">
    Copy the job URL and share it through any channel you prefer.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
