Bookmarks let you save items across the platform for quick access later. You can bookmark courses, roles, and career tracks from anywhere in the Talent Marketplace.

## How to bookmark an item

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the item">
    Navigate to any course, gigs, or job openings in the platform.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click the bookmark icon">
    Click the **bookmark icon** on the item's card or detail page. The icon will fill in to confirm the item has been saved.

    [Insert image 14 here]
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access your bookmarks">
    Click the [bookmark icon](https://app.betalented.ai/bookmarks) in the top navigation bar to view all your saved items in one place.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Managing bookmarks

Your bookmarks page displays all saved items organised by type.

[Insert image 15 here]

* **My Learnings** — Saved learning programmes you plan to enrol in
* **My Gig Opportunities* — Projects you are interested to explore
* **My Jobs** — Saved job openings

To remove a bookmark, click the **bookmark icon** again on the item to deselect it.

<Tip>
  Use bookmarks to build a shortlist of your learning/career development plan before deciding which to enrol in.
</Tip>
