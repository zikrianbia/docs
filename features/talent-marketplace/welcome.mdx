The Talent Marketplace is your central hub for exploring career pathways, understanding skill gaps, and accessing personalised learning recommendations. This module helps employees and managers make informed, skills-based career decisions within the organisation.

## Homepage Overview

When you log in, you will see a comprehensive snapshot of your professional standing:

* **Profile Summary**: Quick view of your current status.
* **Skills**: Your current selected skills.
* **Recommendations**: Dynamic role and learning suggestions based on your profile.

<Tip>
  You can toggle between roles to filter recommendations based on your current role or selected aspirations.
</Tip>

## Career Insights

The Career Insights section helps you understand your current position and potential career progression.

* **Insights for You**: View recommended career pathways, see required core skills for your current role, and identify skill gaps. You can also review your **match score** for each role and explore recommended skills to strengthen your profile.
* **My Aspirations**: Explore roles you are interested in pursuing. Toggle between different aspired roles, view required skills vs. your current skills, and explore similar career tracks.
* **Explore Other Roles**: Browse the full catalogue of roles within the organisation. View responsibilities, skill requirements, and filter by criteria to find the perfect next step in your career.

## Learn Module

The Learn module provides personalised course recommendations designed to bridge your skill gaps.

* **Explore Recommendations**: View internal and external courses tailored to your current role or aspirations. See provider details, duration, and the specific skills you will acquire.
* **Browse All Courses**: Use powerful filters and search tools to explore the full catalogue of available learning programmes.
* **My Enrolments**: Keep track of your learning journey with visibility into your enrolled and completed courses.

## My Profile

Your profile is the engine that drives your recommendations. Keep it updated to ensure the best results.
### Skills & Development
* **My Skills**: View and manage your current skills, upload a new CV, or update your proficiency levels.
* **Skills Journal**: A digital timeline recording every new skill added and proficiency update made to your profile.
### For Managers
* **My Team**: Managers can view team members’ skill profiles, identify collective skill gaps, and share recommendations. You can recommend up to three skills at a time to support team development.

## Additional Features

* **Bookmarks**: Save courses for future reference.
* **Notifications**: Stay updated on new recommendations and bookmarked items.
* **Help**: Access support or report issues directly from the profile menu.
