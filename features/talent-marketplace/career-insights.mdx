The Career Insights module helps you visualise your career trajectory, identify skill gaps, and explore roles that match your capabilities. It is divided into three views.

## Insights for you

This view provides a personalised snapshot of your current career standing based on your role and skills profile.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-15.png)
</Frame>

You can see:

- **Career pathway** — A visual flowchart showing your current role, potential next steps, and how they connect.
- **Match scores** — Each role displays a match indicator (e.g. "Strong", "Medium") based on how closely your skills align.
- **Core skills and gaps** — We identifiy the skills required for your current role are listed, with gaps highlighted so you know where to focus and create your learning plan.

### Quick actions on skills

Click the three-dot menu on any skill pill to:

- Remove the skill from your profile
- Mark it as "I want to develop this skill"
- View associated learning courses
- Update your proficiency level

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-17.png)
</Frame>

<Tip>
  Toggle between your current role and aspired roles to see how your recommendations change.
</Tip>

## My aspirations

Track the roles you are working towards and understand what it takes to get there.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-19.png)
</Frame>

- View up to **3 aspired roles** selected during onboarding
- Compare required skills against your current skill set
- See your **match score** for each aspired role
- Explore **similar career tracks** — alternative pathways based on your background and interests

You can remove/update your career aspirations at any time from the "My aspirations" tab by clicking on the (⋮) icon on the top right corner of the aspired role card.

## Explore other roles

Browse the full list of roles available within the organisation.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-21.png)
</Frame>

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-23.png)
</Frame>

- Search by job title or keyword
- Filter by **Entity, Division, Department, Job Role, Job Profile, and Job Rank Name**
- Discover roles you may not have considered
- View the complete pathway, role descriptions, required skills, and match scores

<Tip>
  Use the "Explore Other Roles" tab to plan lateral moves or explore adjacent career tracks outside your current department.
</Tip>
