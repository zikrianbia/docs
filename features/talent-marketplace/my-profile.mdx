Your profile is the foundation that drives all platform recommendations. Keep it updated for the most relevant career and learning suggestions.

## Profile overview

Access your profile by clicking your avatar in the top-right corner and selecting **My Profile**.

[Insert image 9 here]

Your profile dashboard shows:

* **Personal information** — Name, email, and contact details
* **Profile picture** — Upload or update your photo
* **Professional summary** — A brief description of your background and expertise
* **Talent Future Index (TFI)** — Your readiness score across key categories

---

## Talent Future Index

The TFI measures your readiness across categories like **Digital Readiness** and compares your score against industry benchmarks.

[Insert image 10 here]

* View your scores as bar charts across readiness dimensions
* Compare against your industry average
* See recommended courses to **bridge the gaps** in underperforming areas

<Info>
  The TFI is automatically calculated based on your skills profile and learning activity. Complete more courses and update your skills to improve your scores.
</Info>

## My skills

Manage and refine the skills that define your professional profile.

[Insert image 11 here]

From the **My Skills** section, you can:

* View all your current skills and their sources (CV, manual entry, certified, role-based)
* **Add or remove skills** using the Manage Skills interface
* **Toggle development priorities** — Flag skills you want to actively develop
* **Update proficiency levels** — Adjust your self-assessed proficiency for each skill
* **Upload a new CV** — Re-extract skills from an updated resume

<Tip>
  Skills flagged for development will generate targeted course recommendations in the Learn module.
</Tip>

## Skills journal

The Skills Journal is your personal record of skill growth over time.

[Insert image 12 here]

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a skill">
    Choose a skill from your profile that you want to document progress for.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add an entry">
    Describe how you applied or developed the skill. Include dates and context.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Attach evidence">
    Upload supporting files or paste links to certificates, projects, or reviews that demonstrate your proficiency.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Journal entries build a verifiable timeline of your development — useful for performance discussions and career planning.

---

## Export your profile

You can export your complete skills profile in two ways:

* **Download as PDF** — A formatted document of your skills, proficiency levels, and development history
* **Share as a link** — Generate a shareable link to your profile summary

  This section is only available to users with a **Manager** role.
</Note>

Managers can access **My Team** to oversee their direct reports' skills and development.
