## My team/Team & members (manager view)

<Note>
  This section is only available to users with a **Manager** role.
</Note>

Managers can access **My Team** to oversee their direct reports' skills and development.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-35.png)
</Frame>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="users" title="Team skills overview">
    View a consolidated view of your team's skill profiles and development priorities.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="chart-bar" title="Skill gaps and overlaps">
    Identify collective skill gaps across the team and discover **skill gaps**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="arrows-repeat" title="Knowledge sharing">
    See which team members can mentor others based on proficiency differences. The platform highlights mentoring opportunities where one member's strength matches another's gap.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="paper-plane" title="Recommend skills">
    Suggest up to **3 skills** at a time to individual team members to support their development plans.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
