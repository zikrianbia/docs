The Team & Members feature gives managers a bird's-eye view of their team's skills, strengths, and development areas. From here, you can explore individual profiles, identify skill gaps, and send targeted recommendations to support your team's growth.

## Help your team grow and explore

As a manager, you can recommend specific skills for learning, gigs, or jobs opportunities to your team members.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-25.png)
</Frame>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Share team recommendations">
    From the Team & Members page, click **Start recommending**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select skills to recommend">
    Inside the [share recommendations](https://app.betalented.ai/manager/team-share-recommendations) page, search and select the skills you want to recommend. For each skill, you can:

    - Add a personalised note explaining why you think the skill is relevant
    - Attach recommended learning opportunities or gig opportunities
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose recipients">
    Select which team members will receive the recommendations. You can choose one or more members from your team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and submit">
    Review all the information and click **Submit**. The selected team members will be notified by email.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  When recommending skills, include a note explaining the context — for example, how the skill connects to an upcoming project or the team member's career aspirations.
</Tip>
