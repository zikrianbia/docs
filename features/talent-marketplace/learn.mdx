The Learn module provides personalised course recommendations to bridge your skill gaps and support your career growth. You can browse internal and external learning programmes, enrol in courses, and track your progress.

## Explore recommendations

When you open the Learn module, you will see curated course recommendations organised into categories:

[Insert image 5 here]

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Courses for your current role" icon="briefcase">
    Courses mapped to the skills required for the role you currently hold. These help you strengthen your core competencies and close skill gaps.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Courses for your aspired roles" icon="bullseye-arrow">
    Courses aligned to the skills needed for the roles you selected as career aspirations. These help you prepare for your next career move.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Skills you want to develop" icon="chart-line">
    Courses linked to skills you have flagged for development from your Career Insights or profile settings.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Popular courses" icon="fire">
    Trending and highly rated courses across the platform.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  Use the **Internal / External** toggle to filter between company-hosted courses and third-party provider content.
</Tip>

## Browse all courses

For a broader view, switch to the **Browse All** tab to explore the full course catalogue.

[Insert image 6 here]

You can filter by:

* **Skills to acquire** — Find courses that teach specific skills
* **Content provider** — Filter by provider (e.g. Coursera, Udemy, internal LMS)
* **Language** — Course delivery language
* **Level** — Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced
* **Instructional type** — Self-paced, instructor-led, blended
* **Price** — Free, paid, or subsidised

## Course details

Click on any course card to view detailed information.

[Insert image 7 here]

Each course page includes:

* Provider name and logo
* Duration and format
* Skills you will acquire upon completion
* Course description and learning outcomes
* Enrolment action button

---

## Enrol in a course

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a course">
    From the recommendations or browse tabs, click on a course you are interested in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review details">
    Check the course description, duration, provider, and the skills it covers.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enrol">
    Click **Enrol** to register for the course. You will be redirected to the provider's platform if it is an external course.
  </Step>
</Steps>
