The Learn module provides personalised course recommendations to bridge your skill gaps and support your career growth. You can browse internal and external learning programmes, enrol in courses, and track your progress.

## Explore recommendations

When you open the Learn module, you will see curated course recommendations organised into categories:

<Frame>
  ![Image 20](/images/image-20.png)
</Frame>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="briefcase" title="Courses for your current role">
    Courses mapped to the skills required for the role you currently hold. These help you strengthen your core competencies and close skill gaps.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="bullseye-arrow" title="Courses for your aspired roles">
    Courses aligned to the skills needed for the roles you selected as career aspirations. These help you prepare for your next career move.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="chart-line" title="Skills you want to develop">
    Courses linked to skills you have flagged for development from your Career Insights or profile settings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="fire" title="Popular courses">
    Trending and highly rated courses across the platform.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  Use the **Internal / External** toggle to filter between company-hosted courses and third-party provider content.
</Tip>

## Enrol in a course

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a course">
    From the recommendations or browse tabs, click on a course you are interested in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review details">
    Check the course description, duration, provider, and the skills it covers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enrol">
    Click **Enrol** to register for the course. You will be redirected to the provider's platform if it is an external course. [image x]
  </Step>
</Steps>

## My enrolments

If you have internal courses, you can track your learning journey from the **My Enrolments** tab.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-22.png)
</Frame>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="In progress">
    View courses you are currently enrolled in, along with progress indicators showing how far along you are.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Completed">
    View courses you have finished and the skills you gained from them.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
