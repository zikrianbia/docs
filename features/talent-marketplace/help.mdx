## Accessing help

## Submit feedback or a support ticket

If you cannot find an answer in the knowledge base:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the feedback form">
    Click **Send Feedback** or **Report an Issue** from the Help section.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Describe your issue">
    Provide a clear description of the problem or feedback. Include relevant details such as the page you were on and what you expected to happen.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit">
    Click **Submit** to send your ticket to the support team. You will receive a confirmation and follow-up via email.
  </Step>
</Steps>
