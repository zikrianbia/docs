Notifications keep you informed about relevant activity across the platform — from new course recommendations to role matches and system updates.

## Accessing notifications

Click the **bell icon** in the top navigation bar to open the notifications panel.

[Insert image 16 here]

## Types of notifications

You may receive notifications for:

* **Learning recommendations** — New courses suggested based on your profile updates
* **Role matches** — New roles that align with your skills and aspirations
* **Skill updates** — Reminders to review or update your skills profile
* **Team activity** (managers) — Updates related to your team members' profiles
* **System announcements** — Platform updates and maintenance notices

## Managing notifications

* Click on a notification to navigate directly to the related item
* Notifications are marked as **read** once opened
* Older notifications remain accessible in the panel for reference

<Tip>
  Check your notifications regularly to stay on top of new learning opportunities and career matches.
</Tip>
