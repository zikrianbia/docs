The Talent Attraction module is your central hub for recruitment. From here, you can create job posts, review matched talent, invite candidates to apply, and manage your hiring pipeline.

## Dashboard

When you open Talent Attraction, you see a dashboard listing all your job posts with key metrics at a glance.

[Insert image 19 here]

The dashboard displays:

* **Active jobs counter** — Total number of currently active job posts
* **Job cards** — Each card shows the job title, employment type, region, posting date, match and applicant counts, and the assigned hiring manager
* **Status indicators** — Quickly identify which posts are active, pending approval, or inactive

---

## Filtering and sorting

Use the **Filter & Sort** controls to narrow down your view.

[Insert image 20 here]

Available filters include:

| Filter | Description |
|:---|:---|
| **Employer** | Filter by company name or UEN |
| **Job Owner** | See posts assigned to a specific job owner |
| **Posted Date** | Filter by date range |
| **Skills** | Find jobs requiring specific skills |
| **Status** | Toggle between Active and Inactive posts |

You can also sort by **Latest** or **Oldest** posting date.
