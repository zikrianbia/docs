You can manage your job posts at any time from the Talent Attraction dashboard. Access management actions through the **three-dot menu** (⋮) on any job card.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-61.png)
</Frame>

## Edit a job post

Update the details of an existing job post.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the menu">
    Click the three-dot menu (⋮) on the job card and select **Edit**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make changes">
    Update any fields — job description, skill requirements, salary range, or other details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Submit your changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Duplicate a job post

Create a new job post pre-filled with details from an existing one.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Duplicate">
    Click the three-dot menu (⋮) on the job card and select **Duplicate**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review pre-filled data">
    A new job form opens with all fields populated from the original post.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Modify and post">
    Adjust any details as needed (e.g. title, vacancies, dates) and submit the new post.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Duplicating is the fastest way to create similar roles across different locations or teams.
</Tip>

---

## Extend a nearly expired job

When a job post is nearing its expiry date, We will send a reminder email on a monthly basis. To Extend a job post, you have to go to **Edit job post** \> **Additional details** section \> Edit the **Job posting period**.

  ![Image](/images/image-62.png)
</Frame>

---

## Close a job post

When a role has been filled or is no longer needed, close the post to remove it from active listings.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Close">
    Click the three-dot menu (⋮) on the job card and select **Close**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Confirm the closure. The post will be moved from **Active** to **Inactive** in your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Closed job posts and their applicant data are retained in the Inactive section for future reference.
</Note>
