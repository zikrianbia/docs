<Note>

</Note>

The Skills Inventory provides a comprehensive list of all the experiences, competencies, and professional skills of current employees — designed for organisational planning and workforce capability analysis.

## All Skills view

When you open the [Skills Inventory](https://app.betalented.ai/skills-inventory), you will see a department-level overview of skills across the organisation. Use the top-level Search Skills bar to find specific skills across the entire organisation.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-40.png)
</Frame>

You can click on the specific row and column to see the who had the skills and the proficiency level in specific departments.

<Tip>
  Use the Skills Inventory to identify subject matter experts across the organisation when staffing new projects or forming cross-functional teams.
</Tip>
