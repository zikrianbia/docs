Talented is the talent operating system for skills-based hiring, mobility and workforce planning. It brings workforce insights, internal mobility, talent attraction and redeployment into one integrated platform for enterprises, government agencies and education partners.

## Business benefits for organisations

### 1. Faster, Higher-Quality Talent Decisions

Talented replaces manual, keyword-based processes with contextual, skills-based matching.

- **Shortlist the right candidates faster** using real skill relevance, not job title proxies
- **Reduce hiring and redeployment cycle time**
- **Improve quality of hire** and role fit

**Outcome:** Less time wasted screening. Better hires and placements first time.

### 2. Unlock Internal Mobility and Retain Talent

Most organisations underestimate their internal talent.

- Talented makes internal skills visible and actionable.
- Matches employees to internal roles, gigs, mentorships and learning pathways
- Reveals hidden skills and adjacent career opportunities
- Supports structured progression across job architectures

**Outcome:** Higher retention, stronger engagement, lower replacement cost.

### 3. Execute Redeployment and Career Transition at Scale

When roles change or demand shifts, speed and structure matter.

- Talented enables enterprise-wide redeployment and transition programmes.
- Identifies internal redeployment options before external hiring
- Maps skill gaps to reskilling pathways
- Supports outplacement and workforce resilience initiatives

**Outcome:** Reduced redundancy risk, better employee outcomes, reputational protection.

### 4. Make Workforce Planning Data-Driven

Move from reactive HR planning to proactive workforce strategy.

- Real-time skill demand, supply and gap analytics
- Organisational skill heatmaps by role, team and division
- Scenario modelling aligned to business strategy

**Outcome:** Leadership teams plan with evidence, not intuition.

### 5. One Platform Across the Talent Lifecycle

Most organisations use disconnected tools for hiring, mobility, learning and planning. Talented unifies them.

- Single skills ontology across attraction, mobility and planning
- Consistent data, metrics and reporting
- Enterprise-grade security and compliance

**Outcome:** Lower system complexity, better data integrity, clearer ROI.
