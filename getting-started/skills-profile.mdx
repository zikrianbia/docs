After defining your role, the next step is to build your skills profile. This allows the platform to understand your capabilities and generate personalised role and learning recommendations.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Retrieve Your Skills">
    You can populate your skills in two ways:

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-63.png)
    </Frame>
    - **Option A: Upload Your CV**: Upload your CV to automatically extract and populate your existing skills.
    - **Option B: Connect to Certified Skills**: For Singapore Citizens and Permanent Residents, you can retrieve verified skills from your SingPass account.

    Once retrieved, your skills will appear in your skills profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Retrieved Skills">
    After retrieval, you will be able to:

    - View the number of skills extracted
    - See all populated skills in your profile
    - Remove any skills that are not relevant (click the “×” icon)

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-64.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refine Your Skills">
    If you did not upload a CV or connect certified skills, this section will initially be empty. **You must add at least one skill to proceed.**

    You can refine your profile by:

    - Confirming skills retrieved from your CV or certified records
    - Adding recommended skills based on your selected role
    - Adding skills manually

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-65.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Recommended Skills">
    The platform will suggest skills relevant to your selected role. You can click on a suggested skill to add it, or select **“View all skills”** to see more.

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-65.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Skills Manually">
    If a specific skill is not listed, you can search and add it manually. Start typing the skill name and select the match from the list.

    <Frame>
      ![Image](/images/image-66.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Why This Step Matters

Your skills profile is the foundation for:

- Accurate skill gap analysis
- Career pathway recommendations
- Personalised learning suggestions
- Internal mobility opportunities

The more accurate and complete your skills profile, the more relevant your recommendations will be. Click **Continue** once you have completed your skills profile.
