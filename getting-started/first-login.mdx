When you sign in for the first time, you will be guided through a short onboarding wizard.

![Onboarding Wizard](https://d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/699e6a820ff5bfbeb0e81a01/images/69a670f29fee90aab19e90f4/file-Vs4179u1Na.png)

### Why This Is Important

The onboarding process helps the platform:

* Understand your skills and experience
* Create your skills profile
* Identify any skill gaps
* Capture your career aspirations
* Recommend relevant roles and learning opportunities

<Tip>
  For the best experience, complete all steps in the onboarding wizard. The more accurate your profile, the more personalised your recommendations will be.
</Tip>

We suggest that you upload your CV so we can identify your key skills and experience to build your skill profile automatically.
