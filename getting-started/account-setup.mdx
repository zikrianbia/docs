This guide will walk you through the process of activating your account and setting up your secure login credentials.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Receive Your Activation Email">
    To begin, check your inbox for an activation email sent by JobTech.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/image-53.png"
        alt="Image"
        title="Image"
        className="mx-auto"
        style={{ width:"90%" }}
      />
    </Frame>
    Open the email titled "Activate your account" and click the **Activate your account** button. This will redirect you to the secure password creation page.

    <Note>
      If you do not see the email, please check your spam folder or reach out to [support@jobtech.co](mailto:support@jobtech.co).
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Your Password & Sign in">
    Enter your password in the **New Password** field. Once the password is setup, you can now access the platform.
  </Step>
</Steps>
