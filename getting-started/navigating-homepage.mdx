Your homepage is your central hub for career insights, role recommendations, and learning opportunities tailored to your unique skills profile.

## Dashboard overview

When you log in, you'll be greeted by your personalized dashboard. Here’s a breakdown of what you’ll find:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Career Insights" icon="chart-line">
    View market trends and demand for your core skills.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Role Recommendations" icon="briefcase">
    Explore roles that match your current proficiency levels.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Learning Path" icon="graduation-cap">
    Discover courses to bridge your skill gaps.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Saved Items" icon="bookmark">
    Quickly access your bookmarked roles and learning content.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Key sections

### 1. Skill match score
At the top of your dashboard, you'll see your overall match score for your target role. This gives you a quick snapshot of how close you are to your professional goals.

### 2. Recommended roles
Based on your skills profile, the platform identifies roles you're a strong candidate for, as well as "aspirational" roles that you're close to achieving.

### 3. Skill gaps & learning
This section highlights the specific skills you need to develop to move to the next level in your career. Each gap is linked to curated learning resources.

<Tip>
  Keep your skills profile updated to ensure your homepage recommendations remain relevant and accurate.
</Tip>
