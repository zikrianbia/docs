### Main Navigation Bar

Located at the top of your screen, the main navigation bar is your gateway to all core features:

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-2.png)
</Frame>

 1. **Home:** View your personalized dashboard, including skill summaries and course recommendations.
 2. **Career Insights:** Access comprehensive overviews of skills needed for specific roles and visualize your career pathways.
 3. **Learn:** Explore internal and external courses curated to bridge your identified skill gaps.
 4. **Jobs:** View and apply for internal job openings within your organization.
 5. **Gigs:** Browse short-term projects and internal assignments.
 6. **Mentorship:** Connect with mentors or register as a mentee to facilitate knowledge sharing.
 7. **Hub menu:** Serves as the central switching station within the platform, allowing you to move between different features of Talented.
 8. **Bookmarks:** Quick access to all the courses and job openings you have saved for later.
 9. **Notifications:** Stay updated on application statuses, new recommendations, and platform alerts.
10. **Profile menu:** Place where you can manage your account and administrative settings.

> **Note:** If certain modules like Gigs or Mentorship are not visible, please contact our customer success representative to enable them or [Schedule a demo](https://calendly.com/jobtech/30min).

### The Hub Menu

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-4.png)
</Frame>

The **Hub** (represented by the grid icon) acts as a central switching station between different TalentTrack+ modules:

- **Talent Marketplace:** Access personalized learning and career development tools based on your unique profile.
- **Talent Attraction:** (For HR/Managers) Manage job postings and acquire new talent through skills-based matching.
- **Workforce Redeployment:** Strategically reassign and reallocate employees to different roles or projects.
- **Workforce Planning:** Align your human capital with future business goals and forecasting needs.
- **Analytics Dashboard:** Provide real-time insights into enterprise usage, user engagement, and skills
- **Skills Inventory:** Provide an overview of workforce skills, enabling enterprise to identify strengths, gaps, and development opportunities
- **User Directory:** A centralized, digital database used to view employee information and provide a real-time, skills-first overview of your organization's entire workforce.
- **Workforce Intelligence:** Place where you can download all your organisation's insight for Talented Platform.

### Your Personal Menu

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-6.png)
</Frame>

Located on the far right of the navigation bar, the **Profile Menu** allows you to manage your account and administrative settings:

- **View Profile:** Update your professional summary, skills journal, and profile picture.
- **Help:** Submit a support request or report technical issues.
- **Admin Panel:** (For Administrators) Access user management, organization settings, and job architecture configurations.
- **Skills Journal:** A personalized timeline that automatically documents your professional development, including every skill added, verified, or updated in your profile over time.
- **Knowledge Base:** This menu will redirect user to this guide website.
- **Team & members:** This feature allows you to view your team's skills profiles, track their onboarding progress, and share personalized learning recommendations to support their professional growth.
- **Sign Out:** Securely exit your TalentTrack+ session.
